Theme settings allow designers to provide an easy way for any user to customize the look and feel of their shop without having to delve into HTML or CSS code. Please see our help page on theme settings to learn more about how you can make your theme customizable.

General

Logo Options

Maximum logo dimensions are 940px wide by 300px high.
Your uploaded file will be resized to fit within those constraints.
Need a full-width banner? Upload an image with a width of 940px, and a height of 300px or less.
Money options
Although you will be able to display prices on your store front in different currencies, Shopify will still process orders in your shop's currency.
Separate your currency codes with a space. Use the ISO 4217 3-letter currency code. Click here for a complete list of currency codes. Example: CAD USD.
* For this functionality to work in your shop, you are required to edit your money formats as per these instructions (“Do tell where the money is”).
